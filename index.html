Personal Profile

Zhigang Lu is a Post-doctoral Research Fellow with Optus Macquarie University Cyber Security Hub, Macquarie University, working closely with Prof. Dali Kaafar and Dr. Hassan Asghar.

Prior to that, he was a Ph.D student in School of Computer Science, the University of Adelaide since Feb. 2016, where he was jointly supervised by Prof. Hong Shen and Prof. Michael Sheng. From Jul. 2013 to Aug. 2015, he obtained his M.Phil degree in Computer Science at the University of Adelaide, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Shen and A/Prof. Nickolas Falkner. From Sept. 2007 to Jul. 2011, he received his B.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology at Xidian University.

Research Interests

Differential privacy, Adversarial machine learning, Secure multi-party computation, Cybersecurity, Information theory
