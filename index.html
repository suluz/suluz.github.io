Personal Profile

Zhigang Lu is a Post-doctoral Research Fellow with Optus Macquarie University Cyber Security Hub, Macquarie University from Jan 2020, working closely with Prof Dali Kaafar and Dr Hassan Asghar.

Prior to that, he obtained his PhD (supervised by Prof Hong Shen and Prof Michael Sheng) and MPhil (supervised by Prof Hong Shen and A/Prof Nickolas Falkner) degrees from the University of Adelaide and BEng degree from Xidian University all in computer science.

Research Interests

Differential privacy, Adversarial machine learning, Secure multi-party computation, Cybersecurity, Information theory
