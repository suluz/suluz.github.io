Personal Profile

From Jan/2020, I am a Post-doctoral Research Fellow with Optus Macquarie University Cyber Security Hub, Macquarie University. My new homepage with Macquarie University will be available soon.

Zhigang Lu is currently a final year Ph.D student in School of Computer Science, the University of Adelaide. He is jointly supervised by Prof. Hong Shen and Prof. Michael Sheng.

From Jul. 2013 to Aug. 2015, he obtained his M.Phil degree in Computer Science at the University of Adelaide, under the supervision of Prof. Hong Shen and A/Prof. Nickolas Falkner. From Sept. 2007 to Jul. 2011, he received his B.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology at Xidian University.

Research Interests

Differential privacy, Adversarial machine learning, Cybersecurity, Secure multi-party computation
