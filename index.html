You'll Never Walk Alone

Personal Profile

Zhigang Lu is a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with Macquarie University Cyber Security Hub, Macquarie University from Jan 2020, working closely with Prof Dali Kaafar and Dr Hassan Asghar.

Prior to that, he obtained his PhD (supervised by Prof Hong Shen and Prof Michael Sheng) and MPhil (supervised by Prof Hong Shen and A/Prof Nickolas Falkner) degrees from the University of Adelaide and BEng degree from Xidian University all in computer science.

He also worked as a Data Scientist in the industry for three months.

Research Interests

Differential privacy, Secure multi-party computation, Information theory, Adversarial machine learning, Transfer learning
